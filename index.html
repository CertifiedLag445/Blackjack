Objective

  • Get as close to 21 as possible without going over — and beat the dealer.

Game Setup

  • Place your bet, then cards are dealt. The dealer's second card is hidden until your turn ends.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10): face value.
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): worth 10.
  • Aces: 1 or 11, whichever is better.

Player Actions

  • Hit — Draw another card.
  • Stand — Keep your current total.
  • Double Down — Double bet, take one card, then stand.
  • Split — Split a pair into two separate hands.

Dealer Rules

  • Dealer hits on 16 or below, stands on 17 or above.

Winning

  • Beat the dealer without busting.
  • If dealer busts, all non-bust hands win.
  • A tie returns your bet (push).
  • Blackjack pays 3:2.

Multiple Hands

  • Select 1–4 hands on the left before dealing.
  • A pulsing gold dot shows which hand is active.
  • Complete each hand in turn before the dealer plays.
